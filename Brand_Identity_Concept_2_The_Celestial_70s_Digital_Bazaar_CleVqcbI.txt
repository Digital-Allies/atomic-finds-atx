# Concept 2: The "Celestial 70s" Digital Bazaar Brand Board

## Vibe & Atmosphere
Playful 1970s energy, highly interactive, tactile, and charming. The website should feel like browsing a curated vintage market — not a sterile e-commerce site. Invites conversation, negotiation, and community.

## Color Palette
- **Celestial Yellow** — #F5C842 (Hero accent)
- **Amber Orange** — #D4822A
- **Earth Brown / Bamboo Tan** — #D4A853
- **Woven Moss** — #556B4A
- **Rattan Black** — #2A2017
- **Deep Charcoal** — #1E1E1E
- **Bone White** — #F0E8D8

## Typography
- **Primary:** Chunky, rounded retro-display font with nostalgic 1960s–70s character for brand name and headlines
- **Secondary:** Elegant cursive script for sub-taglines ("Curated Rattan & Bamboo")
- **UI/Body:** Clean modern sans-serif for web legibility — buttons, navigation, body text

## Visual Language
- Wicker and bamboo textures translated into web backgrounds and section dividers
- Atomic orbit motifs as UI accents and loading animations
- Retro-styled custom navigation icons (not generic UI — feels handcrafted)
- "Make an Offer" interactive bazaar interaction model
- Dark mode option with celestial yellow neon glow accents

## Digital Experience
- Interactive bazaar feel — browsing should feel personal, not transactional
- Responsive logo system: full badge → wordmark → AF monogram across breakpoints
- "Restored in Austin TX" stamp badge as trust/authenticity marker
- Wicker texture overlays on card backgrounds and section breaks

## Mood
Digital night-market meets vintage boutique. Warm retro energy with modern interactivity. The charm of haggling at a flea market, translated to screen. Golden hour warmth even in pixels.
