The Concepts

Below are the two distinct archetypes we've developed for Atomic Finds. You can view the full representations of these concepts within the Asset Library section below.

Direction 01

The "Fran & Mabel" Rattan Retreat

Quirky, avant-garde bohemian. This concept transforms the boutique into a sun-drenched restoration studio. It is approachable, warm, and deeply personal.

  • Textures: Rattan, Macramé, Raw Bamboo.
  • Atmosphere: Austin flea-market golden hour.

Direction 02

The Celestial 70s Digital Bazaar

Playful, interactive, and charming. The website becomes a curated marketplace under the stars, utilizing straightforward interactions to build community.

  • Style: Neon glowing orbits, wicker overlays.
  • Tech: High-interactivity without the maze.
