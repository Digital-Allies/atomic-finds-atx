# Concept 1: The "Fran & Mabel" Rattan Revival Retreat

## Vibe & Atmosphere
Quirky, avant-garde bohemian, approachable, and warm. An inviting, community-driven space that encourages personal connection — not a stiff, formal gallery. Think sun-drenched 1970s bohemian sunroom meets curated Austin vintage bazaar.

## Color Palette
- **Celestial Yellow** — #F5C842 (Hero accent)
- **Earth Brown / Bamboo Tan** — #D4A853
- **Forest Teal** — #4A6B6B
- **Woven Moss** — #55684A
- **Charcoal** — #1E1E1E
- **Bone White** — #F0E8D8

## Typography
- **Primary:** Chunky, rounded retro-display font (Cooper Black / thick slab serif family) for "ATOMIC" and headlines
- **Secondary:** Elegant, fluid cursive script for "Finds" and accent callouts
- **Body:** Clean, warm sans-serif for readability

## Visual Language
- Tactile hand-woven rattan textures, bamboo grain, macramé knots
- Atomic starburst and orbital ring motifs bridging the "Atomic" brand name with vintage craft
- Herringbone weave patterns, sunburst rays, groovy 70s wave lines
- Hand-drawn monolinear icon style — warm, not clinical

## Key Textures & Materials
Woven rattan, raw bamboo, macramé fiber, celestial yellow upholstery, sun-bleached wood, dried botanicals

## Mood
Sun-drenched, nostalgic, curated. "High-end thrift" meets restoration studio. Warm afternoon light, hanging plants, woven everything. Feels like the best vintage shop you've ever walked into.
